AI PING PONG GAME











Creator: Soumya Singh || Github: Soumya Singh

Instructions

  • First keep your laptop screen straight.
  • Move yourself approximately 3-4 feet away from your laptop.
  • Move your right wrist in an ascending and descending manner. A red dot should appear on your wrist.
  • Now press the play button, and again move yourself 3-4 feet away from your laptop.
  • The red paddel is your paddel. It will move as per the movement of your wrist.
